What is Phroogal?

Phroogal is answers and access to financial knowledge. We’re crowd-sourcing financial information to help you make better financial decisions.

Phroogal connects you to money-savvy friends, financial experts and the most relevant financial resources.

Phroogal was founded in 2013 with a vision to improve the lives of millions of people by removing the information silos and disrupting the search monopoly on financial knowledge.

Our Mission

It’s simple. Our mission is to increase your knowledge of money and connect you to resources so you can live a financially stress-free and richer life.

Solving The Biggest Financial Problem

Financial illiteracy is a big issue in America and many programs are aimed to improve the financial wellbeing of millions of Americans. But, why hasn’t financial education made a bigger impact?

The problem is that programs and products do not address the way people learn money behaviors - through social interaction and search engines.

After all the classroom training and mobile apps downloaded, people still turn to search to find answers to their money questions. They are then left to sift through countless links, advertisements and search engine optimized information that is often irrelevant and unreliable.


Our Approach

Phroogal is search, questions and answers and a directory listing of the best financial resources and tools. We enable you to connect with the collective knowledge of many to help you make better financial decisions.

You can ask anonymous questions and share your financial wisdom. You can discover great new resources and tools. You’ll find the just-in-time financial education you need when you need it.

The Phroogal Lifestyle

Mindset has a lot to do with financial wellbeing. We live by the Phroogal lifestyle. The Lifestyle is about living richer lives through financial understanding, collaboration and money management.

We believe:

  • It’s about the relationships with family, friends and strangers.
  • It’s about experiences and not material things.
  • It’s about how money is used and not how much more to make.
  • It’s about spending consciously with savings priority.
  • It’s about acquiring and sharing financial knowledge.
  • It’s about using available resources to help live the dream lifestyle.

Our Team

Jason Vitug
Founder and CEO
@jasonvitug

Jason Vitug is the founder of Phroogal. He holds an MBA and B.S. in Finance from Norwich University and Rutgers Business School.

Having worked in the financial services industry for 10 years, Jason championed workplace financial literacy nationwide. In 2012, he left Silicon Valley to travel the world and while sitting on an 8th century temple in Myanmar had an epiphany. He was living a childhood dream and it was then he asked himself, “How can I help others live their dream lifestyle?” That question led to Phroogal.

Originally from New Jersey, Jason grew up in an inner city and learned first-hand how smart money management can help one live richer lives. He had his fair share of money shortfalls, credit problems and mounting student loan debt. It was through education that allowed him to life the Phroogal lifestyle.

Jason believes people can live richer lives through money-savviness. His personal mission is to increase financial knowledge by enabling social collaboration online to allow people to live richer lives offline.

Jason lives a balanced life enjoying extreme outdoor activities, exploring natural and historical wonders, staring up at the night sky, practices yoga and meditation.
